About PLAYED

About us

PLAYED addresses the global health issue by spreading both awareness and evidence-based information about internet, net-game and game addiction. Also called “video and game addiction” The latest research collected from the whole world, edited and made available to parents, children and to the public who search information about this growing global matter.

Widespread and objective

PLAYED relies on the idea that by letting research institutes, therapists, corporations and donors work together, we can make attractive, widespread and at the same time reliable and objective site for information. Our Mission

PLAYED basic idea is that by use the “PLAYED” application and its website the user helps to strengthen overall health of the population by aligning themselves to the group that is or is at risk to develop an addiction of online gaming or unsound data usage. There may be computer games, social media, or, for example, onlinepoker. By working preventively through public education and through “PLAYED” given user ideas and suggestions on how one can try to prevent an unhealthy lifestyle. Public health encompasses all aspects of health, mental, social and also physical. PLAYEDs basic aim is thus to prevent someone becoming a net addict or addicted to gambling by providing information on the symptoms.

Our challenge

PLAYED main focus is to provide understandable information and evidence based methods to parents and other adults about game addiction and “Video and game addiction”.
