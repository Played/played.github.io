Help a friend or relative:

By using the PLAYED application on your phone you are: given simple and easy understandable information and useful conversations keys to prevent constant conflicts with your child.

Easy to download:

If you have an iPhone or iPad just download PLAYED to your phone from the App Store.

About PLAYED parent:

PLAYED parent section, is for those who know something is not right with their child’s computer use. You need to do something but you do not know what to say or do. With a smile “what to say and do day by day” PLAYED provides the user with useful methods and a new way to talk about your child’s unhealthy internet use.

About PLAYED Adult Addiction:

Are you an adult and find it hard to stop playing poker or online gaming or the like. You understand that it is something you begin to have difficulty maintaining control over. PLAYED may be a first step to understanding what really is going on. With a simple and easy to understand 8 days of “do it yourself” method, PLAYED might help you to take the right steps.

PLAYED In schools:

By using “PLAYED Parents” as a teacher in schools. You are given a discussion support to develop a dynamic group discussion. Questions and answers that will bring thoughts that young people may be able to identify themselves with. As computers have moved into homes and into handheld devices, the use of social media and online gaming has increased dramatically worldwide. Unsound and unhealthy use is therefore something “PLAYED” can help you work preventively with a group or in class.
