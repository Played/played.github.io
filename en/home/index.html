PLAYED

RAISING AWARENESS OF VIDEO AND NETGAME ADDICTION

Played provides you with “easy to understand” information in its mobile application that can be downloaded to your mobile phone. The content of the app can be used by parents, adults and groups, such as school classes.

REDUCES GAMING

By providing simple conversation techniques to parents of children and youth who play too much computer games or using their computer in an unhealthy manner. Played mobile application can also be used by adults who suspect they are addicted to online-gaming or gambling.

PREVENTS ADDICTION

By working proactively = prevention and provide information about what is happening before it becomes a problem.

EVIDENCE-BASED INTERVENTION

Intervention is when a third party is helping to identify the right kind of help if you or a relative or someone you know is addicted to online-gambling or video and net-game addiction.

CUSTOMIZES SOFTWARE

Played offer the gaming industry to implement Project PLAYED and its information into their products, such as computergames and mobilegames. By supporting project Played we can together reach out with necessary information and tools to help as many people as possible.

SHARES KNOWLEDGE

Played building a global knowledge bank concerning gambling, online gaming addiction and Video and game addiction.
