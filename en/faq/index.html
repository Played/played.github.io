PLAYED is an information service for parents and their children or other adults. We address health issues concerning video and game addiction, internet and net- game addiction, by providing evidence-based and clever information about this growing problem.

PLAYED provides accurate and easily understandable information for parents and other adults about game addiction and “Video and game addiction” by providing and spreading the highly accessibly application and innovative content worldwide.

PLAYED application and this website help you with both arguments and useful information.

PLAYED content

All information and its content are verified by physical therapist and psychologist Owe Sandberg. He treats game addicts and video and game addicts in Sweden. Collecting global research on game addiction, net-game addiction and video and game addiction. Editing information and reports and make it understandable for everybody. Publishing interesting articles and information on the PLAYED website and PLAYED application.

How is the user data stored and treated by PLAYED?

Is it shared with other companies? PLAYED never share your individual information to third parties unless you yourself have provided this information in response to an advertisement or offer from our partners. PLAYED has the right to compile information from users of the www.played-web.com and its application on an aggregated but not personal level, presenting data as statistics and activity.
