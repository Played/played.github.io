FAQ and other information

PLAYED is a information service for parents and their children or other adults. We address health issues concerning video and game addiction, internet and net- game addiction, by provide evidence-based and clever information about this growing problem.

PLAYED provide accurate and easy understandable information for parents and other adults about game addiction and “Video and game addiction”. By provide and spread the highly accessibly application and innovative content worldwide. PLAYED application and this website help you with both arguments and useful information.

How is the user data stored and treated by PLAYED? Is it shared with other companies?

PLAYED never share your individual information to third parties unless you yourself have provided this information in response to an advertisement or offer from our partners. PLAYED has the right to compile information from users of the www.played-web.com and its application. On an aggregated but not personal level, present data as statistics and activity.
