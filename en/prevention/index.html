What is prevention and how does PLAYED contribute?

What does prevention work mean?

PLAYED basic idea is that by use the “PLAYED” application the user helps to strengthen overall health of the population by aligning themselves to the group that is or is at risk to develop an addiction of online gaming or unsound data usage. There may be computer games, social media, or such as. By working preventively through public education and through PLAYED, the user is given access to ideas and suggestions on how one can try to prevent an unhealthy lifestyle.

Public health encompasses all aspects of health, mental, social and also physical.

PLAYEDS basic aim is thus to prevent someone becoming a net addict addicted to gambling by providing information about the symptoms.
